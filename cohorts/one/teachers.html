Teachers

Erin Angell

eangell [at] pausd [dot] org

Erin Angell is the English teacher for SJP Cohort #1. After 13 years of teaching, 7 of them at Paly, she has extensive experience teaching different grade levels and English elective classes. Her enthusiasm as a teacher and advisor has led her to explore unconventional methods of teaching, and she continues to challenge her students and their knowledge of the world around them through the creation of the Social Justice Pathway.

Eric Bloom

ebloom [at] pausd [dot] org

Eric Bloom, a Paly history teacher and advisor with 17 years of teaching experience, is the history teacher for Cohort #1. His style of teaching leads to many discussions and projects in class, and he enjoy creating unique lesson plans and assignments for his students. His inventiveness and love of social justice led him to collaborate with Ms. Angell to create the Social Justice Pathway at Paly.
